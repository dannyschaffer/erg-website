Frequently asked

Landlord debt collection questions, answered.

  • How is this different from a generic collection agency for landlords?

    ERG works exclusively with property portfolios — resident balances, move-out debt, eviction costs, and aging accounts — instead of applying a generic consumer-debt process to landlord accounts.

  • Does a landlord collections agency partnership replace our internal team?

    No. ERG works alongside internal property teams as a specialist recovery partner, so staff can stay focused on leasing, operations, and resident experience.

    FDCPA, Regulation F, NMLS licensing, and state-by-state licensing discipline guide every account and every resident interaction.
