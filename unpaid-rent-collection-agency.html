The unpaid rent problem property teams actually have.

Unpaid rent rarely arrives as a single clean balance. It shows up as a current resident falling behind, a former resident who moved out owing rent and damages, or an eviction judgment that never converted into cash. Elite Recovery Group (ERG) treats each of those as a distinct account type instead of running every unpaid rent account through the same script.

Resident balances

A current resident's rent that's gone past due.

Move-out debt

Rent, damages, and cleaning charges left behind after move-out.

Eviction costs

The legal costs of an eviction the judgment never actually paid for.

Aging portfolios

Unpaid rent that's aged past the point your team has time to chase.
